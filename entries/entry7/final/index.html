Last Quarter Moon
This occurs on Monday, November 5th, 2023, at 3:37 AM EST.
Early Monday morning, the Last Quarter Moon is exact, when the Sun in Scorpio forms a square with the Moon in Leo. The Last Quarter Moon phase points to some sort of crisis of consciousness. After basking in the awareness symbolized by the full light of the Moon at the time of last week’s Full Moon, we disperse our knowledge and come to a point when we need to sort out what works for us — and what doesn’t. This is in preparation for next week’s New Moon when something new is born once again.
